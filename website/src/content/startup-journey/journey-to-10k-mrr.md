## 🎯 What We're Building

**My Robots** - An AI-powered multi-agent platform for SEO automation, content generation, and marketing workflows.

**Core Product:**
- **SEO Robot:** 6-agent system for research, strategy, content creation, and optimization
- **Newsletter Robot:** Automated newsletter generation with Exa AI research
- **Article Generator:** Competitor analysis + original content creation

**Differentiation:**
- Open source approach (vs proprietary black boxes)
- Multi-agent architecture (vs single AI models)
- Cost optimization ($0-50/mo vs $200-400/mo competitors)
- Technical depth (graph theory, PageRank, semantic cocoons)

## 💡 Strategy & Assumptions

### Pricing Strategy

**Tier 1: Starter** - $49/month
- 50 articles/month
- 1 user
- Email support
- **Target:** Freelancers, solo marketers

**Tier 2: Pro** - $99/month
- 200 articles/month
- 3 users
- Priority support
- API access
- **Target:** Small agencies, growing teams

**Tier 3: Agency** - $149/month
- 500 articles/month
- 10 users
- Dedicated support
- White-label option
- **Target:** Marketing agencies, enterprise teams

**Assumptions:**
- 70% Starter, 25% Pro, 5% Agency
- Average plan: $62/month
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 20%
- Monthly churn: 5%

### Growth Assumptions

**Months 1-3 (Jan-Mar):**
- Growth: 100% month-over-month (small base)
- Channel: Organic SEO (80%), word-of-mouth (20%)
- CAC: <$50 (content marketing)

**Months 4-6 (Apr-Jun):**
- Growth: 50% month-over-month
- Channel: SEO (60%), partnerships (30%), paid (10%)
- CAC: <$75

**Months 7-12 (Jul-Dec):**
- Growth: 25% month-over-month
- Channel: SEO (50%), paid (30%), partnerships (20%)
- CAC: <$100

### Risk Factors

**High Risk:**
- ⚠️ SEO takes 3-6 months (revenue delay)
- ⚠️ Competitive pressure (established players)
- ⚠️ API cost scaling (Groq, Exa AI)

**Medium Risk:**
- ⚠️ Churn rate higher than expected
- ⚠️ Feature parity with competitors
- ⚠️ Support load overwhelming

**Mitigation:**
- Pre-launch email list building
- Focus on unique differentiation
- Free tier APIs + caching strategies
- Exceptional onboarding + docs
- Roadmap transparency
- Fast response times
