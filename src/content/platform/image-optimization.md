## Global CDN Delivery

Your images deserve the same treatment as Netflix and Spotify content.

### What is CDN Delivery?

A Content Delivery Network stores your images on servers worldwide. When someone visits your site:

- **Traditional hosting**: Image loads from one server (slow for distant visitors)
- **CDN delivery**: Image loads from the nearest server (fast everywhere)

### Speed Benefits

| Visitor Location | Traditional Server | With CDN |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------|
| Same country | 200ms | 50ms |
| Different continent | 800ms | 100ms |
| Remote regions | 2000ms+ | 150ms |

### On-the-Fly Optimization

Our CDN doesn't just store images—it optimizes them in real-time:

- **Format conversion**: Automatically serves WebP or AVIF to supported browsers
- **Quality adjustment**: Balances file size and visual quality per device
- **Smart cropping**: Focuses on the important parts of each image
- **Lazy loading ready**: Perfect integration with modern browsers

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How fast are images generated?

Most requests complete in 30-60 seconds, depending on the strategy chosen and number of images.

### Can I edit images after generation?

Images are delivered as standard files. Download and edit them in any image editor if needed, or regenerate with different settings.

### What image formats are supported?

The CDN automatically serves the best format for each browser:
- **WebP** for Chrome, Firefox, Edge
- **AVIF** for cutting-edge browsers
- **JPEG/PNG** fallback for older browsers

### Do I need my own CDN account?

Yes. We integrate with Bunny.net for CDN delivery. Plans start at affordable rates for most blogs and businesses.

### Will this slow down generation of my articles?

Image generation adds about 30-60 seconds to the content pipeline. The time saved compared to manual image creation is substantial.

### How does this affect my hosting costs?

Images are stored on the CDN, not your server. This typically reduces your hosting bandwidth while adding a small CDN cost that's offset by performance benefits.

### Can I use my own images instead?

Yes. Use the Upload feature to optimize and deliver any existing image through the CDN with all the same performance benefits.

### What about copyright?

Generated images are created for your use. They don't use copyrighted materials or stock photos.
