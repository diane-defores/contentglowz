## 💡 SEO Strategy Details

### On-Page Optimization

**Every Article Includes:**
- ✅ TL;DR at top (quick value)
- ✅ H1 with primary keyword
- ✅ H2/H3 with secondary keywords
- ✅ Meta title (50-60 chars)
- ✅ Meta description (150-160 chars)
- ✅ Schema.org Article markup
- ✅ FAQPage schema (5-7 Q&As)
- ✅ Internal links (3-5 per article)
- ✅ External links (2-3 authoritative sources)
- ✅ Alt text on images
- ✅ Optimized URLs (keyword-rich)

### Content Quality Signals

**Depth Metrics:**
- Average article length: 8,200 words
- Code examples: 15-20 per article
- Tables/comparisons: 3-5 per article
- Real-world examples: 2-3 per article

**Engagement Optimization:**
- Scannable format (short paragraphs)
- Numbered/bulleted lists
- Visual hierarchy (bold, italics)
- Actionable takeaways
- Multiple CTAs (top, middle, bottom)

### Technical SEO

**Site Performance:**
- Core Web Vitals: Target all green
- Page load time: <2 seconds
- Mobile-friendly: Responsive design
- HTTPS: Enabled
- Sitemap: Submitted
- Robots.txt: Optimized

**Structured Data:**
- Organization schema
- Article schema (all posts)
- FAQPage schema (FAQ sections)
- BreadcrumbList schema
- SearchAction schema
