| Factor | Railway | Heroku | Winner |

## 🎯 Strategic Decisions

### Pricing Strategy Evolution

**Initial (Month 2):**
- Free: 100 API calls/month
- Starter: $29/month (1,000 calls)
- Pro: $99/month (5,000 calls)

**Current (Month 6):**
- Free: 500 API calls/month
- Starter: $49/month (5,000 calls)
- Pro: $149/month (20,000 calls)
- Enterprise: Custom

**Results:** 2.5x ARPU, 40% better free-to-paid conversion.

### Product Focus Changes

**Original Plan:** SEO content generation for SMBs

**Actual Need:** SEO research automation for agencies

**Pivot Timeline:**
- Month 1-2: Built content generation features
- Month 3: Discovered agencies need research automation
- Month 4: Built research analyst agent
- Month 5: Product-market fit achieved
- Month 6: Focused entirely on agency tools

**Lesson:** Listen to customers, not your initial vision.

## 💡 Lessons Learned

### Technical Lessons

1. **Infrastructure choice matters** - Railway saved us $30/month and hours of deployment pain
2. **Dependency optimization compounds** - 50% fewer deps = 40% faster builds
3. **API design is critical** - Version your APIs from day one
4. **Monitoring is non-negotiable** - You can't optimize what you can't measure

### Business Lessons

1. **Niche down before scaling up** - Agencies > SMBs for our product
2. **Free tier is a growth engine** - 4.2% conversion from free to paid
3. **Content > Ads for B2B** - 4x better CAC from blog content
4. **Transparency builds trust** - Building in public creates loyal customers

### Personal Lessons

1. **Burnout is real** - Take weekends off, set boundaries
2. **Co-founder alignment crucial** - Regular strategy meetings prevent drift
3. **Customer conversations gold** - Every customer call reveals product insights
4. **Celebrate small wins** - 6 months is a marathon, not a sprint
