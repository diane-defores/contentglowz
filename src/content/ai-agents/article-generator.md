# Article Generator: From Competitive Insights to Original Content

**TL;DR:** The Article Generator crawls competitor websites, identifies topics they cover that you don't, and generates original SEO-optimized articles to fill those gaps—turning competitive research into actionable content.

---

## The Content Gap Problem

Your competitors rank for topics you haven't covered. You know you should analyze their content, identify opportunities, and write articles to capture that traffic. But who has time?

**The reality:**
- Manually analyzing competitor sites takes hours
- Identifying meaningful gaps requires pattern recognition
- Writing quality content for each gap takes even longer
- By the time you finish, competitors have moved on to new topics

---

## How the Article Generator Works

### Step 1: Competitive Crawling

The robot crawls competitor websites and extracts their content in clean, readable format. Not just titles—full articles with structure, headings, and topics.

> **Clean Extraction**
>
> Unlike simple web scrapers, the crawler extracts semantic content—understanding what's the main article vs. navigation, ads, and sidebars. This gives accurate topic and keyword data.

### Step 2: Content Analysis

With competitor content extracted, the robot performs semantic analysis:
- What topics do they cover?
- What keywords are they targeting?
- How is their content structured?
- What's their word count and depth?

### Step 3: Gap Identification

Comparing competitor coverage to your existing content reveals gaps:
- Topics they cover that you don't
- Keywords they rank for that you're missing
- Questions they answer that you haven't addressed

> **Gap Prioritization**
>
> Not all gaps are worth filling. The robot prioritizes based on search volume, keyword difficulty, and alignment with your topical focus. High-opportunity gaps surface first.

### Step 4: Original Article Generation

For each prioritized gap, the robot generates an original article:
- Title optimized for search and clicks
- Logical structure with proper heading hierarchy
- Comprehensive coverage of the topic
- Natural keyword integration
- Internal linking recommendations
- Schema markup for search engines

### Step 5: SEO Validation

Before output, every article passes quality checks:
- Word count meets minimum thresholds
- Keyword density is natural (not stuffed)
- Readability score is appropriate for your audience
- Meta title and description are optimized
- Internal links connect to your existing content

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the generated content original or just rewritten?**
A: Original. The robot analyzes competitor topics and structure, but generates new content from scratch. Uniqueness scores consistently exceed 90%.

**Q: How many competitors can I analyze at once?**
A: As many as needed. Analyzing 3-5 competitors gives a comprehensive view of topic coverage in your space.

**Q: Will generated articles match my brand voice?**
A: Generated articles follow SEO best practices and clear writing principles. You should edit for brand voice during review before publishing.

**Q: How do I prioritize which gaps to fill?**
A: The robot scores gaps by opportunity—balancing search volume, keyword difficulty, and topical relevance. Start with high-opportunity, lower-difficulty gaps.

**Q: Can I generate articles without competitor analysis?**
A: Yes. If you already know the topics you need, provide them directly and skip the competitive crawl step.

**Q: How does this integrate with my content calendar?**
A: Generated articles go to your content queue, where the Scheduler Robot handles optimal publish timing and deployment.
